## Project Overview

Portland Fuel has been invited to present **Part 2** of CITT's "2026 Fuel Briefing" webinar.

- **Event:** CITT 2026 Fuel Briefing (3-part webinar)
- **Portland Fuel slot:** Part 2, ~30 minutes
- **Presentation date:** ~April 17, 2026
- **Format:** Online webinar

## Audience

Senior Canadian logistics decision-makers:
- ~60% transportation service providers (carriers: trucking, rail, marine, air)
- ~40% shippers (retail, mining, agriculture, CPG, pharma, petrochemicals, food & beverage)
- Small portion: consultants, academics, tech/law service providers
- Level: Sr. Manager through C-suite

## Portland Fuel's Scope (Part 2)

Per the CITT brief:
- Detailed look at current Canadian and global fuel markets
- How various stakeholders are behaving
- Scenario modelling and informed forecasts
- Fuel hedging and other risk mitigation strategies

## Fuel Types in Scope

- Crude oil (WTI, Brent, WCS)
- Diesel / ULSD (primary focus — most relevant to transport audience)
- Jet fuel / Jet A
