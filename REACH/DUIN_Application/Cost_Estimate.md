# UK REACH Registration Cost Estimate - Straightforward Application

**Substance:** Urea (CAS 57-13-6, EC 200-315-5)
**Tonnage Band:** 100-1000 tonnes per year
**Registration Deadline:** October 27, 2028
**Application Type:** Straightforward (via consortium, well-studied substance)

---

## Executive Summary

**Estimated Total Cost (Straightforward Consortium Route): £18,000 - £35,000**

This estimate assumes:
- ✓ Joining an existing UK REACH Urea consortium
- ✓ Existing data available from EU REACH registrations
- ✓ Minimal new testing required
- ✓ Standard uses of Urea (no unusual applications)
- ✓ Straightforward exposure scenarios
- ✓ Your company classified as Large Enterprise (for HSE fees)

**Timeline:** 18-24 months from consortium joining to registration submission

**Payment Schedule:** Spread over 2026-2028

## Cost Breakdown by Category

### 1. Consortium Membership Fees

**What it covers:**
- Access to shared toxicological and ecotoxicological data (Annexes VI-IX)
- Joint registration coordination
- Lead registrant services
- Data rights for UK REACH registration
- Consortium management and administration

**Estimated Cost:**

| Tonnage Band | Typical Consortium Fee Range | Mid-Point Estimate |
|--------------|-----------------------------|--------------------|
| 100-1000 t/yr | £8,000 - £18,000 | **£13,000** |

**Assumptions:**
- Joining established consortium (lower cost than forming new one)
- Data package already complete or near-complete
- Standard cost-sharing formula based on tonnage
- Urea is common substance with multiple registrants (economies of scale)

**Payment Schedule:**
- Initial membership fee: 40-50% (£5,200 - £9,000)
- Milestone payment(s): 30-40% (£3,900 - £7,200)
- Final payment at submission: 10-20% (£1,300 - £3,600)

**Notes:**
- Earlier joiners often pay less
- Costs may be lower if multiple companies from consortium already completed UK REACH registrations
- Some consortiums charge annual maintenance fees (typically £500-£2,000/year)

### 3. HSE Registration Fee

**What it covers:**
- UK REACH registration processing by Health and Safety Executive
- Regulatory review and evaluation
- Registration number issuance

**Fee Schedule (Effective April 1, 2025):**

| Company Size | Definition | Registration Fee |
|--------------|------------|------------------|
| **Large Enterprise** | ≥250 employees OR >€50m turnover | **£2,222** |
| Medium Enterprise | <250 employees AND ≤€50m turnover | £740 |
| Small Enterprise | <50 employees AND ≤€10m turnover | £399 |
| Micro Enterprise | <10 employees AND ≤€2m turnover | £57 |

**Source:** The REACH etc. (Fees) (Amendment) Regulations 2025

**For Budget Planning:** Assume **£2,222** (large enterprise rate) unless you qualify as SME

**Payment Timing:** Payable at time of registration submission (2028)

**Important Notes:**
- This is a one-time registration fee
- Significantly reduced from previous fees (was £8,185 for 100-1000 t/yr before April 2025)
- No annual maintenance fees currently required by HSE
- SME fee reductions require proof of company size at time of submission

## Why This is a Straightforward Application

**Factors that make Urea registration straightforward:**

1. **Well-Studied Substance**
   - Urea has been used commercially for decades
   - Extensive existing data from EU REACH registrations
   - Published scientific literature widely available
   - No data generation required (or minimal)

2. **Low Hazard Profile**
   - Not classified as CMR (Carcinogenic, Mutagenic, Reprotoxic)
   - Not PBT/vPvB (Persistent, Bioaccumulative, Toxic)
   - Not a substance of very high concern (SVHC)
   - Standard risk management measures

3. **Common Uses**
   - Fertilizer, AdBlue, industrial chemicals - all well-documented
   - Exposure scenarios standardized
   - No unusual or proprietary applications requiring custom CSA

4. **Established Consortiums**
   - High-volume substance likely has existing UK REACH consortium
   - Data packages likely complete or near-complete
   - Economies of scale due to multiple registrants

5. **Regulatory Precedent**
   - EU REACH registrations provide template
   - HSE familiar with substance
   - Low risk of unexpected HSE questions or challenges
