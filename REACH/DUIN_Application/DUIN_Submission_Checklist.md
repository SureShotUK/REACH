## Phase 6: Interim Period Management (2025-2028)

### Ongoing Import Operations

- [ ] Confirmed legally able to continue importing under DUIN deferral
- [ ] Updated internal compliance documentation to reflect DUIN status
- [ ] Briefed procurement team on continued ability to import
- [ ] Maintained records of all imports during DUIN period

### Prepare for Full Registration (Before October 27, 2028)

**Year 1 (2025-2026):**
- [ ] Research UK REACH Urea consortiums
- [ ] Contact REACH consultancies for consortium membership guidance
- [ ] Join industry associations (AIC, CIA) for regulatory support
- [ ] Begin budgeting for registration costs (£10,000-£30,000 consortium; or £70,000-£350,000 individual)
- [ ] Monitor HSE guidance updates on UK REACH registration process

**Year 2 (2026-2027):**
- [ ] Join Urea consortium (if identified)
- [ ] OR Engage REACH consultant to prepare individual registration
- [ ] Begin gathering required data (Annexes VI, VII, VIII, IX)
- [ ] Prepare Chemical Safety Assessment (CSA)
- [ ] Draft registration dossier using IUCLID software

**Year 3 (2027-2028):**
- [ ] Finalize registration dossier
- [ ] Complete all data requirements
- [ ] Conduct internal review of dossier completeness
- [ ] Pay HSE registration fee (£2,222 for large enterprise; lower for SMEs)
- [ ] **Submit full UK REACH registration by October 27, 2028**

### Annual Review Tasks

**To be completed every year until registration:**

- [ ] Review import volumes annually
- [ ] Confirm tonnage band remains 100-1000 t/yr (or adjust if necessary)
- [ ] Update stakeholders on registration preparation progress
- [ ] Monitor HSE announcements for any deadline changes
- [ ] Check for available UK REACH Urea consortiums
- [ ] Review budget allocation for upcoming registration costs
