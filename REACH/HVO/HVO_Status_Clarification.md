# HVO DUIN Status Clarification

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: If we don't have to register for REACH to import Urea why do we have to register for HVO?**
- **A:** As we were importing Urea in the 2 years prior to 1st Jan 2021 we qualify for DUIN (Downstream User Import Notification) deferment meaning we do not have to register under UK REACH until 27th October 2029, unfortunately we do not have the same status for HVO as we were not importing prior to 2021.

**Q: Do we qualify for DUIN as a company or per substance?**
- **A:** Per substance. Our DUIN for Urea only covers Urea.

**Q: Do we have to register before we can import HVO?**
- **A:** YES, if importing from non-GB suppliers. NO, if purchasing from GB suppliers (as we would then be a downstream user).

**Q: Can we defer registration because we are DUIN for Urea?**
- **A:** NO. DUIN for Urea does not give us any deferral for HVO.

**Q: Is there time to import before we have to register?**
- **A:** NO grace period for new substances. We must register BEFORE importing ≥1 tonne/year from non-GB sources.

---

### OPTIONS:

1. Try to find UK based importers and suppliers of HVO and exclusively use them for our supply.
- This will mean we do not need to register under UK REACH at all and we would be classed as a downstream user.

2. **Recommended Action** Start the process to register for UK REACH, begin with the Article 26 Inquiry, join a consortium and progress to full registration. This will allow us to import HVO ourselves and/or we could continue to buy from other UK suppliers.
- The Article 26 Inquiry does not commit us to anything further and we should gain a list of companies that are registered to import HVO into the UK already.

---

## Legal Compliance Warning

⚠️ **CRITICAL:** Importing HVO from non-GB sources without a valid UK REACH registration is a breach of UK REACH regulations.

**Consequences:**
- Enforcement action by HSE
- Potential fines
- Inability to legally place HVO on market
- Business disruption

**Source:** <a href="https://www.hse.gov.uk/reach/about.htm" target="_blank">UK REACH Explained</a>
