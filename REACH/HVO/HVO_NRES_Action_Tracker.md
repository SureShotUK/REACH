# HVO UK REACH — NRES Registration Action Tracker

**Substance:** Renewable hydrocarbons (diesel type fraction) | EC 700-571-2 | CAS 928771-01-1
**Pathway:** NRES (New Registration of an Existing Substance)
**Tonnage band:** 10–100 tonnes/year
**Full registration deadline:** 27 October 2030
**Article 26 Inquiry ref:** 0000025852-5 (accepted ✅ — 2026-06-26)

---

## When Can We Start Importing?

Once HSE processes the member dossier and issues a UK REACH registration number (~15 working
days after submission), we are legally registered and may begin importing HVO above 1 tonne/year.

The full 2030 registration is needed to **continue** importing beyond that deadline — not to start.

**Recommended:** Before first import, email ukreachitsupport@defra.gov.uk to confirm the
registration number issued from the member dossier is sufficient to begin importing under NRES.
Quote inquiry ref 0000025852-5 in that email.
