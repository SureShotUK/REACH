# HVO UK REACH Consortium Registration - Action Plan

## Why Registration is Required

1. **DUIN** (Downstream User Import Notification) **is Substance-Specific**: Our existing DUIN for Urea provides NO coverage for HVO. DUIN status is per substance, not per company.

2. **No Grace Period**: New substances require registration BEFORE importing. There is no grace or deferment period for substances not covered by an existing DUIN.

3. **Potential Customers in the Wings** We may suffer reputational damage if we say we are able to import HVO then it transpires that we cannot.

**Conclusion**: We must register under **UK REACH before we can legally import HVO into the UK**.

**HVO is already registered under UK REACH** which means we can join the existing **HVO consortium** to obtain registration rather than having to be the lead registrant.

---

## Action Steps

### Step 1: Submit Article 26 Inquiry (Can do ourself)

**Purpose**: Request contact details of existing HVO registrants from HSE

**Timeline**: HSE typically responds within 15 working days

**Cost**: Free

**Added Benefits** We may see all companies currenly registered to import / manufacture HVO in the UK, which could lead us to alternative suppliers. If we used these suppliers exclusively **there would be no need to register under REACH**.

---

### Step 2: Contact Lead Registrant (May need consultant)

**Actions**:
- Contact Lead Registrant using details from Article 26 response
- Express interest in joining consortium
- Request Letter of Access (LoA) to registration data
- Negotiate consortium membership terms and fees

**Considerations**:
- Consortium fees vary by tonnage band (10-100 tonnes/year: typically £1,500-£5,000+)
- Letter of Access costs depend on data sharing arrangements
- We will only really know what this entails when we get to this stage as every consortium can be different.

---

### Step 3: Submit Joint Submission (Likely need consultant)

**Requirements**:
- Complete IUCLID 6 Cloud dossier as consortium member
- Include Letter of Access references to shared data
- Submit through Comply with UK REACH portal
- Pay HSE registration fee (£399 Small SME rate)

**Complexity**: High - consortium submissions require:
- IUCLID 6 technical expertise
- Understanding of data sharing requirements
- Coordination with Lead Registrant
- Compliance with REACH technical documentation standards

**Recommendation**: We try to complete the process in house but we have already made connections to consultants who can help us if we need them in this process.
