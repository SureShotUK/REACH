## Data Waiver Statement

We are submitting this member dossier under the New Registration of an Existing
Substance (NRES) pathway for UK REACH.

The substance — Renewable hydrocarbons (diesel type fraction), EC 700-571-2,
CAS 928771-01-1 — was registered under EU REACH before 1 January 2021. As an NRES
registrant, we are not currently in a position to provide all relevant test summaries
for the information requirements applicable to our tonnage band (10–100 tonnes per year).

We will make every effort to negotiate with other members of the substance group and
will subsequently either:

(a) provide the required data ourselves and submit as lead registrant; or

(b) obtain a Letter of Access to the joint registration data and become a member of
    the joint registration.

We commit to submitting a full registration dossier that complies with all information
requirements for the 10–100 tonnes per year tonnage band before midnight on
**27 October 2030**.
