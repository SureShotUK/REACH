## ONGOING COMPLIANCE ACTIVITIES

### Whenever Purchasing Urea

**Every time you purchase or receive Urea:**

- [ ] **Verify shipment from approved supplier** with valid registration
- [ ] **Check SDS included** with shipment or already on file
- [ ] **Confirm product matches** registered substance (CAS/EC number)
- [ ] **Inspect packaging** for proper labeling
- [ ] **Record receipt** in inventory management system

### If Changing Suppliers

**Complete this checklist if considering a new Urea supplier:**

- [ ] **Confirm supplier is GB-based** (not just GB distributor)
- [ ] **Request UK REACH registration proof** before first order
- [ ] **Verify registration number** with HSE or consultant
- [ ] **Confirm tonnage band** sufficient for our volume
- [ ] **Obtain current SDS** and compare to existing
- [ ] **Verify uses covered** by new supplier's registration
- [ ] **Update internal records** with new supplier information
- [ ] **Communicate uses** to new supplier
- [ ] **File all documentation** for new supplier

**Do NOT purchase from new supplier until registration verified.**

### Supply Chain Communication

**Communicate to supplier when:**

- [ ] **Your uses of Urea change** (new processes, applications)
- [ ] **You discover new hazard information** about Urea
- [ ] **An incident occurs** involving Urea
- [ ] **Adverse health/environmental effects** observed
- [ ] **You need updated or additional information** from supplier

**Document all communications with supplier in compliance files.**

### If Supplying Urea or Urea-Containing Products to Customers

**If you sell or transfer Urea or products containing Urea:**

- [ ] **Provide SDS** to customers (UK REACH compliant version)
- [ ] **Communicate safe use information** per Article 32
- [ ] **Pass along use restrictions** or conditions from supplier
- [ ] **Include UK REACH registration information** if requested
- [ ] **Maintain records** of downstream communications

## INCIDENT REPORTING

**If an incident involving Urea occurs (spill, exposure, injury, environmental release):**

### Immediate Actions

1. [ ] **Respond to incident** per emergency procedures
2. [ ] **Ensure safety** of personnel and environment
3. [ ] **Follow SDS emergency procedures**
4. [ ] **Contact emergency services** if required

### Reporting

Within 24-48 hours:

- [ ] **Document incident** (date, time, description, outcome)
- [ ] **Notify supplier** of incident and any findings
- [ ] **Report to HSE** if required by incident severity
- [ ] **Report to other authorities** (environmental, health) if required
- [ ] **Update risk assessment** if incident reveals new information

### Follow-Up

- [ ] **Investigate root cause**
- [ ] **Implement corrective actions**
- [ ] **Update procedures** to prevent recurrence
- [ ] **Additional training** for affected staff
- [ ] **Document lessons learned**

**Incident Log:** Maintain a log of all Urea-related incidents, no matter how minor.

## RED FLAGS - COMPLIANCE ISSUES TO ESCALATE

**Escalate immediately to management and legal/regulatory advisor if:**

- [ ] **Supplier unable to provide** UK REACH registration proof
- [ ] **Supplier registration expired** or revoked
- [ ] **Supplier registration tonnage** lower than our purchase volume
- [ ] **HSE enforcement action** against supplier
- [ ] **Our uses NOT covered** by supplier's registration
- [ ] **Supplier changes** without proper verification
- [ ] **SDS missing or outdated** beyond reasonable time
- [ ] **Serious incident** involving Urea
- [ ] **HSE inquiry or inspection** related to REACH
- [ ] **Customer complaints** about REACH compliance
- [ ] **Changes to UK REACH** requiring new obligations

**Action if Red Flag Identified:**
1. STOP purchasing from affected supplier (if supplier issue)
2. Contact legal counsel and/or REACH consultant
3. Notify management immediately
4. Document the issue and actions taken
5. Develop corrective action plan
6. Do not resume purchasing until issue resolved

### Appendix A: Urea Substance Information

- **Substance Name:** Urea
- **CAS Number:** 57-13-6
- **EC Number:** 200-315-5
- **Molecular Formula:** CH₄N₂O
- **Molecular Weight:** 60.06 g/mol
- **Common Uses:** Fertilizer, plastics, animal feed, industrial applications

### Appendix B: Key UK REACH Articles for Downstream Users

- **Article 5:** No data, no market principle
- **Article 32:** Duty to communicate information down the supply chain
- **Article 34:** Duty to communicate information up the supply chain
- **Article 38:** Downstream user reports (if using outside supplier's registration)
- **Articles 33-36:** Supply chain communication requirements
