## RED FLAGS - WHEN TO INVESTIGATE FURTHER

Watch for these warning signs that may indicate supplier non-compliance:

- [ ] Supplier unable or unwilling to provide UK REACH registration number
- [ ] Registration number cannot be verified with HSE
- [ ] Tonnage band on registration is lower than your import volume
- [ ] SDS is outdated or not UK REACH compliant
- [ ] Your uses are not covered by supplier's registration
- [ ] Supplier registration has expired
- [ ] Supplier changes and new supplier doesn't provide registration proof

**If any red flags identified, STOP purchasing and investigate immediately.**
