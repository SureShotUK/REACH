## **UK EV MARKET ANALYSIS**

### **Key Finding: Limited Options in Your Criteria Segment**

The combination of **375+ miles range**, **under £65,000**, and **sub-5.5 second 0-60** is extremely rare in the UK market. Most manufacturers optimize for two of these three factors, not all three.

### **The Price-Performance-Range Triangle**

- **Long range + affordable = slow performance** (VW ID.7 Pro S, Mercedes CLA)
- **Long range + fast = expensive** (Mercedes EQS, Porsche Taycan, BMW iX)
- **Fast + affordable = limited range** (Tesla Model 3 Performance, Ioniq 5 N)

### **Chinese Manufacturers Disrupting Premium Segment**

The MG IM5 demonstrates that Chinese manufacturers are delivering exceptional value by breaking the traditional premium pricing model. They're offering long range AND performance at mid-market prices.

### **2026 Market Outlook**

Several manufacturers are introducing updated models in 2026 with improved battery technology, but most still fall short of the 375-mile threshold at the sub-£65k price point. The market is slowly improving but your requirements remain challenging.

## **IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS**

### **Research Limitations**

1. **Specification Volatility:** EV specifications, pricing, and availability change frequently. All data current as of November 2025 but must be verified before purchase.

2. **Model Year Transitions:** Some 2026 models may have updated specifications not yet published.

3. **WLTP vs. Real-World Range:** WLTP figures are laboratory tests under optimal conditions. Real-world range varies significantly based on:
   - Driving style (motorway vs. city)
   - Weather conditions (cold weather significantly reduces range)
   - Use of climate control
   - Terrain
   - Actual range may be 20-30% lower than WLTP in worst conditions

4. **Pricing Variability:** UK pricing can include dealer discounts, manufacturer incentives, and regional variations. Always verify final pricing.
