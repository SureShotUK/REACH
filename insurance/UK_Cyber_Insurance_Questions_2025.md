## Essential Questions for Reviewing Cyber Insurance Quotes

### 1 Coverage Scope and Exclusions

1. **"Are there any scenarios involving failures of third-party suppliers or cloud service providers (e.g., MS365, Azure) that would be excluded from coverage?"**
##

2. **"What are the specific conditions or security postures we must maintain to ensure coverage is not voided? For example, if the quote mentions 'high severity and critical patches', what is the defined timeframe?"**
##

3. **"Does the policy cover incidents arising from non-malicious employee error or social engineering?"**
##


### 2 Sub-Limits and Aggregates

4. **"I see numerous sub-limits, such as £50,000 for 'Post Breach Remediation Costs' and £25,000 for 'Claim Preparation Costs'. Are these limits adequate for a business of our size, and can they be increased?"**
##

5. **"Can you clarify how you define a 'single claim' versus 'related claims' to avoid multiple incidents being bundled into one limit?"**
##


### 3 Deductible Structures

6. **"The quote specifies an 'AGGREGATE DEDUCTIBLE: £10,000'. Does this mean we only pay a maximum of £10,000 in deductibles for the entire policy year, regardless of the number of claims?"**
##

7. **"There is also a 'TIME FRANCHISE: 8 hours' for business interruption. Does this mean we bear the first 8 hours of loss for every incident, or is this a one-off for the policy year?"**
##


### 4 Ransomware and Cyber Extortion

8. **"Under 'CYBER EXTORTION', with a limit of £500,000, what is the process for payment? Will the insurer make the cryptocurrency payment on our behalf?"**
##

9. **"What is your stance on ransom payment negotiation? Do you provide expert negotiators as part of the cover?"**
##


### 5 Business Interruption Waiting Periods

10. **"The 'TIME FRANCHISE' is 8 hours. Does the 'INDEMNITY PERIOD' of 12 months begin after this 8-hour waiting period is over?"**
##

11. **"Can the time franchise begin out of hours or is it always within office hours only?"**
##


### 6 Insurer Claims Handling Reputation

12. **"Can you provide statistics on your average time from claim notification to payment for cyber policies?"**
##



13. **"Are there any public case studies that we can look at to get an idea of what has happened to similar / other businesses and associated costs?"**
##
