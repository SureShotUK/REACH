### 3.2 Stair Use — Important Notice

> **Note for PEEP Coordinator:** Portland Fuel's premises at 1 Toft Green are multi-storey. There are currently **no designated refuge points** and **no evacuation chair** on site.
>
> If the visitor **cannot use stairs unaided or with assistance**, their visit must not proceed to an upper floor until one of the following measures is in place:
> - An evacuation chair has been sourced and a trained operator is available, **or**
> - The visit is relocated to the ground floor, **or**
> - A written risk-based decision has been made and approved by Steve Irwin confirming the visit can safely proceed with named, briefed assistants.
>
> Do not allow an upper-floor visit to proceed on the assumption that assistance alone is sufficient — assess the specific capability of named assistants before confirming.

### 3.3 Hearing / Visual Impairment

| Impairment | Action Required |
|------------|----------------|
| Hearing impairment | The fire alarm may not be audible. A named person must be with the visitor at all times to give a physical alert in the event of the alarm. |
| Visual impairment | The visitor must be physically guided to the nearest exit. Do not assume they can follow others. A named escort must be assigned. |

## Recommendations for Site

> **Current gap in provision:** Portland Fuel does not currently have an evacuation chair or designated refuge points. For visitors with significant mobility impairments, particularly those unable to use stairs, this limits the options available.
>
> **Recommended actions:**
> 1. Consider procuring an evacuation chair and training at least two members of staff in its use.
> 2. Identify a suitable area of rescue assistance on each upper floor for use in future.
> 3. Where possible, schedule visits from people with significant mobility impairments to ground floor areas only until the above are in place.
