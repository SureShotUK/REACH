# HSEEA - Health, Safety, and Environmental Compliance Repository

This repository contains regulatory guidance, best practice documents, and compliance resources for HSE (Health and Safety Executive) and EA (Environment Agency) UK regulations.

## Business Context
- **Jurisdiction**: United Kingdom
- **Operations**: Manufacturing/Industrial and Office/Commercial
- **Purpose**: Maintain compliance with UK HSE and EA regulations while following cost-effective best practices

## Repository Structure

### `/regulations/`
Official HSE and EA regulatory documents, guidance notes, and legal requirements.

### `/guidance/`
Industry best practice guides, HSE guidance documents (HSG series), and INDG publications.

### `/assessments/`
Risk assessments, COSHH assessments, environmental impact assessments, and related documentation templates.

### `/compliance/`
Compliance checklists, audit records, and monitoring documentation.

### `/procedures/`
Standard operating procedures, safe systems of work, and emergency response procedures.

### `/reference/`
Quick reference materials, legislation summaries, and decision-making tools.

## Key Compliance Areas

**Health & Safety (HSE):**
- COSHH (Control of Substances Hazardous to Health)
- RIDDOR (Reporting of Injuries, Diseases and Dangerous Occurrences)
- Manual Handling Operations
- Working at Height
- Machinery Safety (PUWER)
- Display Screen Equipment (DSE)
- Fire Safety
- Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- Workplace Health, Safety and Welfare

**Environmental (EA):**
- Waste Management and Duty of Care
- Environmental Permits
- Water Resources and Discharge Consents
- Air Quality and Emissions
- Contaminated Land
- Pollution Prevention and Control

## Usage

This repository serves as a centralized knowledge base to:
1. Store and organize HSE and EA regulatory documents
2. Provide quick access to compliance requirements
3. Support decision-making on safety and environmental matters
4. Balance regulatory compliance with practical, cost-effective solutions
