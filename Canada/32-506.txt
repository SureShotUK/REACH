2. General condition to exemptions from the CFA dealer registration requirement  
         The exemptions in this Part are not available to a person or company if the person or company is registered under the 
         Act and if their category of registration permits the person or company to act as a dealer or trade in the contract for which 
         the exemption is provided.

         with or on behalf of the CFA permitted client, if at the time of the trade all of the following apply: 
         (a)       the trade is in respect of a foreign contract on a non-Canadian exchange;
