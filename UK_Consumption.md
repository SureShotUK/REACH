## 3. Treasury Cost of Removing 1 Pence Per Litre (1 ppl)

**Conversion factors used (as specified):**
- Diesel: **1,183 litres per tonne**
- Petrol: **1,325 litres per tonne**

### Methodology
Annual consumption (tonnes) × litres per tonne = annual litres consumed  
Annual litres × £0.01 = annual cost of 1 ppl duty reduction

---

### Diesel — road (white diesel)

| Step | Calculation | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Annual consumption | 22,697,590 tonnes | — |
| Annual litres | 22,697,590 × 1,183 | **26.85 billion litres** |
| Cost of 1 ppl — **annual** | 26,851,249,370 × £0.01 | **£268.5 million/year** |
| Cost of 1 ppl — **per day** | £268,512,494 ÷ 365 | **£735,650/day** |

### Petrol / Gasoline

| Step | Calculation | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Annual consumption | 11,833,110 tonnes | — |
| Annual litres | 11,833,110 × 1,325 | **15.68 billion litres** |
| Cost of 1 ppl — **annual** | 15,678,870,750 × £0.01 | **£156.8 million/year** |
| Cost of 1 ppl — **per day** | £156,788,708 ÷ 365 | **£429,560/day** |

---

### Summary: Combined cost of 1 ppl reduction on both grades

| Grade | Annual cost (1 ppl) | Daily cost (1 ppl) |
|---|---|---|
| Road diesel (white diesel) | **£268.5 million** | **£735,650** |
| Petrol | **£156.8 million** | **£429,560** |
| **Combined (diesel + petrol)** | **£425.3 million** | **£1,165,210** |

> **Note on red diesel:** Red diesel (agricultural/off-road) carries a lower duty rate (currently 11.14 ppl vs 52.95 ppl for road diesel) and is not normally included in road fuel duty relief calculations. The figures above cover road (white) diesel only. If duty relief were extended to all diesel grades, the additional annual cost per 1 ppl would be a further **£26.1 million/year** (2.206 Mt × 1,183 L/t × £0.01), bringing the all-grades diesel total to **£294.6 million/year**.
