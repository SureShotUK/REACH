# Early Game Gold Making Guide - Skyrim

Here are the top ways to accumulate gold quickly during the early stages of the game:

## 1. Alchemy (The Fastest Method)
Alchemy is widely considered the easiest and fastest way to make money. 
- **How:** Collect ingredients and experiment to find high-value potions.
- **Top Combo:** Blue Mountain Flower + Blue Mountain Flower or Giant's Toe + Wheat (depending on version/mods).
- **Tip:** Focus on "Restore Health" and "Fortify Health" potions as they generally sell for more.

## 2. Transmute Ore & Jewelry
If you can find the **Transmute Mineral Ore** spell, you can turn common iron into gold.
- **How:** Use the spell to turn Iron Ore into Gold Ore. Smelt the gold into ingots at a forge.
- **Profit:** Use those gold ingots to craft jewelry (rings and necklaces) at a forge. Jewelry has a high value-to-weight ratio, making it easy to transport and sell.

## 3. Mining
Mining provides the raw materials for the Transmute method and general smithing.
- **How:** Visit mines such as **Halted Stream Camp** or **Embershard Mine**.
- **Tip:** Always carry a pickaxe and clear out the nearby forts/camps to ensure safe access to the veins.

## 4. Dungeon Looting
The traditional way to earn gold.
- **How:** Clear out early-game dungeons and forts.
- **Tip:** Focus on looting jewelry, potions, and unique weapons. Avoid looting heavy armor or weapons unless you intend to use them or have the carrying capacity, as they take up too much space for their value.

## 5. Smithing (Upgrading Gear)
While it requires an initial investment in materials, upgrading gear increases its value.
- **How:** Use workbenches to improve weapons and armor.
- **Tip:** Focus on items with high base values.

## 6. Enchanting
Once you reach level 100 in a crafting skill or find high-level items, you can begin enchanting.
- **How:** Use Soul Gems to add powerful effects to gear.
- **Tip:** Creating gear with "Fortify Alchemy" or "Fortify Enchanting" allows you to create an exponential loop of value.

## 7. Bounties and Early Quests
Completing early tasks for Jarls or town guards.
- **How:** Take on simple delivery quests or clear out nearby bandit camps.
- **Tip:** These provide a steady stream of guaranteed gold and often reward you with gear you can sell.

## 8. Farming (Hearthfire DLC)
If you have access to the Hearthfire DLC early, farming is a great passive income source.
- **How:** Build a garden at Goldenhills Plantation.
- **Tip:** Plant high-value ingredients like **Mora Tapinella** or **Creep Cluster** and turn them into potions via Alchemy.
