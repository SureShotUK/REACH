# Skyrim Enchanting Guide: Magic User Edition

Enchanting is a critical skill for magic users, allowing you to increase your Magicka pool, regenerate energy faster, and specialize in specific schools of magic. This guide focuses on the early game setup to ensure your mage is powerful and sustainable.

## 1. Best Early-Game Enchantments for Mages

As a magic user, your primary goals are **increasing Magicka** and **reducing the cost of spells**.

### Essential Armor Enchantments
| Item Slot | Recommended Enchantment | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Head** | Fortify [School] of Magic | Reduces the cost of spells for that school (e.g., Destruction). |
| **Chest** | Fortify Magicka / Regen Magicka | Increases your total pool or how fast it refills. |
| **Necklace** | Fortify Magicka / Regen Magicka | The most powerful slot for Magicka boosting. |
| **Ring** | Fortify [School] of Magic | Further reduces spell costs. |
| **Gloves** | Fortify [School] of Magic | Essential for specialized builds. |
| **Boots** | Fortify Magicka / Stamina | Useful for mobility and resource management. |

**Top Priority:** Focus on **Fortify Destruction** or **Fortify Restoration** first, as these are the most used schools in combat. **Resist Magic** is also a high priority for defensive gear.

## 2. How to Start Enchanting

To create an enchanted item, you need three things:
1. **An Item:** A piece of armor or a weapon (non-magical).
2. **A Soul Gem:** A charged gem to provide the energy.
3. **An Enchantment Effect:** An effect you have "learned" by disenchanting other magical items.

### The Workflow:
- **Step 1: Disenchant.** Find a magical item (e.g., a ring that increases Magicka). Use an **Arcane Enchanter** to disenchant it. *Warning: This destroys the item.*
- **Step 2: Charge.** Ensure you have a filled soul gem (see section 3).
- **Step 3: Enchant.** Select your target item $\rightarrow$ select the learned enchantment $\rightarrow$ select the filled soul gem.

---

## 3. Soul Gems: Finding and Filling

Soul gems are the "batteries" for your enchantments. The larger the soul, the more powerful the enchantment.

### How to Fill a Gem:
- **Soul Trap Spell:** Cast the `Soul Trap` spell on a target before killing them.
- **Soul Trap Weapons:** Use a weapon that already has the `Soul Trap` enchantment.
- ** Filling Process:** If you have an empty gem in your inventory, the soul will automatically be captured upon the target's death.

### Finding Gems:
- **Looting:** Check chests in dungeons and bandit camps.
- **Purchase:** Buy them from Court Wizards or the College of Winterhold.
- **Mining:** Occasionally found while mining ore veins.

## 4. Creature Soul Mapping

Not every creature fits into every gem. Use this list to target your farming:

| Soul Gem Size | Typical Creatures | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Petty** | Chickens, Cows, Deer | Extremely easy to find; good for leveling. |
| **Lesser** | Bears, some small monsters | Common in the wild. |
| **Common** | Cave Bears, Chaurus, some Draugr | Found in most early dungeons. |
| **Greater** | High-level Draugr, Trolls | Requires more caution. |
| **Grand** | Mammoths, Giants, Dragons | High power; rare targets. |
| **Black** | Humans, Elves, Orcs, Vampires | **Only** fits in Black Soul Gems. |

---

## 5. Where to Find the Best Enchantments

You cannot "create" new types of enchantments; you must find them in the world.

- **Dungeon Loot:** The best way to discover new effects is to loot everything. Even "bad" gear might have a unique enchantment you can steal via disenchanting.
- **Specialized Vendors:** Court Wizards often sell items with high-tier magic enchantments.
- **The College of Winterhold:** This is the hub for magic users. Explore the library and speak with students to find unique gear.
- **High-Level Enemies:** Defeating powerful enemies (like Draugr Death Chiefs) often rewards you with gear that has superior enchantments.

## Pro Tip: The "Infinite" Loop
Once you have a basic setup, use your **Alchemy** skill to create a **Fortify Enchanting** potion. Drink this right before you use the Arcane Enchanter to significantly increase the strength of the effect you are applying.
