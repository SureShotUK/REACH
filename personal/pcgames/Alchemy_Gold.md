## Notes

- Waterbreathing potions are by far the most valuable potion effect in Skyrim, thanks to Salmon Roe's 15.4x duration multiplier on the Waterbreathing effect.
- The most valuable potion overall is **Garlic + Fungus Stalk + Salmon Roe** (1,587g at Alchemy 15), but before the Rare Curios DLC the top recipe was **Garlic + Nordic Barnacle + Salmon Roe** (1,564g).
- Boar Tusk appears in both the top Fortify Stamina recipe and the top Waterbreathing recipe.
- Many high-value ingredients (Salmon Roe, Garlic, Nordic Barnacle) are not sold by merchants and must be gathered or farmed.
- At Alchemy 100 with the Alchemist perk and full fortification gear, all values can reach 5x the base price.
