# Stones of Barenziah — Complete Location Guide (All 24)

> Verified against [Skyrim Fandom Wiki](https://skyrim.fandom.com/wiki/Stones_of_Barenziah) and the [No Stone Unturned](https://skyrim.fandom.com/wiki/No_Stone_Unturned) Thieves Guild quest walkthrough. All 24 Stones of Barenziah listed below with dungeon location, area description, and precise position within each location.

**What are they?** Unusual gems that only hold value as a complete set of 24. Finding your first triggers the Thieves Guild quest *"No Stone Unturned"*. Return all 24 to Vex in Ragged Flagon to install them in Tolvald's Crown of Barenziah, granting **Prowler's Profit** (x3 gold from locks & pickpocketing).

## Quest Progression Overview

1. **Find any Stone of Barenziah** → Unusual Gem appears in inventory
2. **Speak to Vex** at the Ragged Flagon (or Maul at the Bee and Barb, Riften) → *"No Stone Unturned"* begins
3. **Collect remaining 23 Stones** → All 24 are now in your inventory as "Stones of Barenziah"
4. **Speak to Vex again** → She directs you to Tolvald's Cave (near the Thieves Guild headquarters)
5. **Retrieve the Crown of Barenziah** from Tolvald's Crossing (southeast section — beware trolls and Falmer!)
6. **Return to Vex** → Stones are set into the crown, becoming **Barenziah's Crown**
7. **Reward: Prowler's Profit** perk activated (triple gold from locks & pickpocketing)

| Stone | Special Requirement |

| Thalmor Embassy (#18) | Only accessible during "Diplomatic Immunity" quest for Delphine in Riverwood. Check Elenwen's Solar first; if not there, check Reeking Cave on exit route. |

## Notes

- The Dawnguard and Dragonborn DLCs do **not** add any new Stones of Barenziah beyond these 24.
- Stones stack in inventory while still labeled "Unusual Gem" but become non-stackable once appraised as "Stone of Barenziah." Gather all before getting them appraised to keep them stacked.
