## Ranking Criteria

Potions are ranked by their overall combat and utility value. Tier S potions enable powerful enchanting loops or significantly boost damage-dealing skills. Tier D potions have niche or situational use.

## Best Ingredient Combinations (Top Potions)

### Top 10 Most Valuable Potion Effects:
1. **Fortify Smithing** — Best for crafting and weapon upgrades
2. **Fortify Enchanting** — Enables the powerful enchanting loop
3. **Fortify Destruction** — Highest damage multiplier
4. **Restore Health + Fortify Health** — Ultimate survivability
5. **Fortify One-handed / Two-handed** — Core melee damage
6. **Fortify Marksman** — Core ranged damage
7. **Fortify Heavy Armor / Block** — Core tank defense
8. **Fortify Sneak** — Core stealth damage
9. **Regenerate Magicka** — Essential for magic builds
10. **Fortify Carry Weight** — Quality of life for exploration

### Most Versatile Ingredients (appear in most top-ranked potions):
- **Glowing Mushroom** — Destruction, Health, Smithing, Shock
- **Blue Mountain Flower** — Conjuration, Health, Poison
- **Hagraven Claw** — Barter, Enchanting, Light
- **Blue Butterfly Wing** — Conjuration, Enchanting, Poison
- **Stoneflower Petals** — Enchanting, Magicka, One-handed
- **Canis Root** — Marksman, One-handed, Poison, Sneak

---

## Notes

- Potion effectiveness scales with Alchemy skill (100 = 50% boost over base).
- With all Alchemy perks + 100% Fortify Alchemy, potions can be **6.25x** stronger for restoration effects, or **5x** for others.
- A 3-ingredient potion can have up to 3 unique effects (not 6).
- The "fortify loop" (enchant alchemy gear → brew fortify alchemy → re-enchant) is the most powerful exploit in vanilla Skyrim alchemy.
