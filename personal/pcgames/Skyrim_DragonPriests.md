# Skyrim Dragon Priests — Complete Location & Mask Enchantment Guide

> Verified against [UESP Wiki](https://uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Priests) and [Skyrim Fandom Wiki](https://skyrim.fandom.com). All 13 collectible Dragon Priest Masks plus 2 named NPCs without masks (15 total priests encountered across Skyrim & Solstheim).

---

## Base Game — 9 Obtainable Masks

| # | Priest Name | Dungeon / Location | Area Description | Mask Enchantment |
|---|-------------|-------------------|------------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Morokei** | Labyrinthian | WNW of Whiterun. Sprawling Nordic ruin and ancient dragon cult dwelling. Final chamber accessible via College of Winterhold quest *"The Staff of Magnus"*. | **+25% Magicka Regeneration** |
| 2 | **Nahkriin** | Skuldafn | Other Realm — accessed only by riding Odahviing during main Dragonborn quest *"Alduin's Wall"*. Temple atop a frozen peak near the portal to Sovngarde. | **+30% Magic Resist** |
| 3 | **Vokun** | High Gate Ruins | West of Dawnstar, NNE of Ustengrav. Nordic ruin with draugr and frost trolls. The Pale hold. | **Conjuration spells cost 25% less** |
| 4 | **Rahgot** | Forelhost | SE of Riften in the mountains. Tomb with crypt, refectory, and battlements. Rift hold. | **Death Storm — electrical explosion on death damaging nearby enemies** |
| 5 | **Otar "the Mad"** | Ragnvald | North of Markarth. Nordic ruin with skull key puzzle (Saerek's & Torsten's) to open the sarcophagus. The Reach. | **+100% Poison Resistance** |
| 6 | **Hevnoraak** | Valthume | SE of Markarth, SW of Rorikstead (west of Gloomreach). Three-chamber Nordic ruin: Vestibule → Catacombs → Cellar. The Reach. | **Necromantic Aura — raises dead draugr to fight for you** |
| 7 | **Krosis** | Shearpoint | In the Pale, accessible by using the Wooden Mask on the Labyrinthian barrow altar. Small hidden chamber; Krosis attacks from range with fireballs. | **Fog of Melody — fear aura; nearby enemies flee (-50% morale)** |
| 8 | **Volsung** | Volskygge | West of Solitude, Haafingar hold. Nordic ruin with a totem puzzle (snake → bear → fox → wolf) on the passages leading to the peak. | **+50% Shock Resistance** |
| 9 | **Konahrik** | Bromjunaar Sanctuary | Only accessible after collecting all other 8 masks + Wooden Mask, placed on the altar in Labyrinthian's barrow (meaning "warlord" in Dragon language). | **Dragon Aspect — shout cost -50% & +25% block** |

## Dragonborn DLC — 4 Masks

| # | Priest Name | Dungeon / Location | Area Description | Mask Enchantment |
|---|-------------|-------------------|------------------|-----------------|

## Notes

- **Total obtainable masks: 13** — 9 base game + 4 Dragonborn DLC. (Lokir of Rorikstead is often listed but he is an NPC from the opening Helgen quest, not a collectible mask.)
- The **Wooden Mask** is found in the barrow inside Labyrinthian's walls and is required to access Shearpoint, Bromjunaar Sanctuary, and Kolbjorn Barrow.
- **Namir** and **Golnak** are encountered as named NPC dragon priests during gameplay but do not have obtainable masks.
- All directional references use cardinal directions from the nearest major city or hold capital.
- Fellglift Keep (Golnak's location) is in Hjaalmarch, south-west of Morthal along the road to Riverwood near the border with The Pale.
