## Notes

**foe → workers** — Mine is initially hostile but workers take over after a related quest is completed:
- **Kolskeggr Mine:** Overrun by Forsworn; original miners return once cleared
- **Redbelly Mine:** Infested with frostbite spiders; workers resume after "Redbelly Mine is Overrun"
- **Sanuarach Mine:** Blocked by Silver-Blood mercenaries; miners take over after the "Sanuarach Mine" quest
- **Soljund's Sinkhole:** Overrun by draugr; workers return once cleared

**Cidhna Mine\*** — Only accessible during/after the quest "No One Escapes Cidhna Mine." The workers are prisoners sentenced to mine silver.

**Lost Prospect Mine** — Abandoned. No workers or enemies; the mine appears played out but three gold veins are hidden in an alcove behind the waterfall (requires jumping or Whirlwind Sprint to reach).

**Hearthfire note** — Hearthfire adds no interior mines. Each of the three homestead build sites (Lakeview Manor, Windstad Manor, Heljarchen Hall) has an outdoor clay quarry and stone quarry for construction materials only.
