# The Alchemy-Enchanting Loop: God-Tier Gear Guide

The "Alchemy-Enchanting Loop" is the most powerful mechanic in Skyrim. It allows you to exponentially increase the strength of your enchantments and potions, eventually leading to gear that can make you nearly invincible or allow you to cast spells for free.

## 1. The Core Concept: The Feedback Loop

The loop is a recursive process where you use one skill to boost the other:
**Fortify Alchemy Gear** $\rightarrow$ **Stronger Fortify Enchanting Potion** $\rightarrow$ **Stronger Fortify Alchemy Gear** $\rightarrow$ **Repeat.**

By repeating this cycle, you can move from a +10% bonus to a +1000% bonus (or higher) in a very short amount of time.

---

## 2. Phase One: Acquiring Fortify Alchemy

Before you can start the loop, you need to learn the **Fortify Alchemy** enchantment.

### How to find it:
Since you cannot craft the effect from scratch, you must find an item that already has it:
- **Looting:** Search chests in dungeons and loot enemies. Look for items labeled "of Alchemy."
- **Shopping:** Check with General Traders and Court Wizards. They occasionally sell rings, necklaces, or bracers of Alchemy.
- **Disenchanting:** Once you find an item with this effect, take it to an **Arcane Enchanter** and disenchant it. This destroys the item but adds "Fortify Alchemy" to your known list of enchantments forever.

---

## 3. Phase Two: The Fortify Enchanting Potion

Once you have the ability to enchant your gear, you need a potion that makes your enchanting more powerful.

### The "Golden" Recipe
For early game, use these ingredients to create a **Fortify Enchanting** potion. The more ingredients you use that share the same effect, the stronger the potion.

| Ingredient | Location | Abundance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blue Butterfly Wing** | Everywhere outdoors; just look for butterflies. | Extreme |
| **Ancestor Moth Wing** | Found in forests and wilderness; kill moths. | High |
| **Hagraven Claw** | Dropped by Hagravens (found in the wild/caves). | Moderate |
| **Snowberries** | Common in snowy regions and tundra. | High |

**Recommended Combo:** `Blue Butterfly Wing` + `Ancestor Moth Wing` + `Hagraven Claw` (or `Snowberries`).

---

## 4. Step-by-Step Execution Guide

Follow these steps precisely to initiate the loop:

1. **Learn the Effect:** Find and disenchant a "Fortify Alchemy" item.
2. **Build the Foundation:** Craft the best Fortify Alchemy gear you can (at minimum: Ring and Necklace). Use the best soul gems you have (Grand or Black).
3. **Gear Up:** Put on all your Fortify Alchemy gear.
4. **Brew the Boost:** While wearing that gear, brew several **Fortify Enchanting** potions using the recipe above. Because you are wearing Alchemy gear, these potions will be significantly stronger than normal.
5. **The Power-Up:** Drink one of your new Fortify Enchanting potions.
6. **Upgrade the Foundation:** While the potion is active, return to the Arcane Enchanter and craft a *new* set of Fortify Alchemy gear. Because you are under the influence of the potion, this gear will be much more powerful than your first set.
7. **The Loop:** Put on the new, stronger gear $\rightarrow$ brew even stronger potions $\rightarrow$ craft even stronger gear.

## Summary Checklist
- [ ] Find/Disenchant Fortify Alchemy item.
- [ ] Craft initial Alchemy Gear (Ring/Necklace).
- [ ] Gather Butterfly Wings, Moth Wings, and Hagraven Claws.
- [ ] Wear gear $\rightarrow$ Brew Fortify Enchanting Potion.
- [ ] Drink potion $\rightarrow$ Craft upgraded Alchemy Gear.
- [ ] Repeat until desired power level is reached.
